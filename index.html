About

Akshay Hiwale is a well learned person who is currently a Lecturer at Nathmal Goenka Law College, Akola, Lawyer and was a pro bono Advocate, who is passionate about imbibing the Constitutional values in young minds. He is a B. Tech (Civil Engineering) graduate from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University (Dr. BAMU) and has done post graduation study in History (i.e M. A) from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) and in Law (i.e LLM - Constitutional law) from Sant Gaadgebaba Amaravati University (SGBAU) as well. He is also qualified for the post of Assistant Professor (NET exam) in History and Law subject. And is currently working in collaboration with Adv. Kushal Jain Sir at Sarthi Law Classes, Akola.
